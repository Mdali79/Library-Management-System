## üìù How to Take Screenshots

### On Mac:
1. Press `Cmd + Shift + 4` for area selection
2. Press `Cmd + Shift + 3` for full screen
3. Press `Cmd + Shift + 4` then `Space` for window capture

### On Windows:
1. Press `Win + Shift + S` for Snipping Tool
2. Or use `Win + PrtScn` for full screen

### Recommended Settings:
- **Format**: PNG (best quality)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher
- **File Size**: Optimize if needed (use tools like TinyPNG)
